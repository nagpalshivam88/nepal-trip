Monsoon Travel Tips

  • Pack smart: quick‑dry clothing, waterproof jacket, backpack rain cover, insect repellent, leech socks.
  • Start drives early – afternoon rains can trigger landslides & delays.
  • Stay flexible: short hikes only if trails are safe. Have indoor backups (museums, cafés, spa).
  • Health & safety: carry a small first‑aid kit, hand sanitizer, and keep electronics in dry bags.
  • Respect wildlife: observe rhinos & crocs from safe distance; skip elephant rides if you prefer ethical tourism.
  • Cash & connectivity: ATMs available in cities; carry some NPR cash in rural areas. Local SIMs (NCELL) offer data.
